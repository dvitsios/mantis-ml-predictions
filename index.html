mantis-ml Prediction Atlas
Overview
◆ mantis-ml Prediction Atlas is a repository for disease-associated gene predictions extracted by mantis-ml.

The atlas currently contains gene prediction results for 3 diseases as well as for a disease-agnostic scenario (GMS or Generic Mantis-ml Score).

◆ You can browse results from each disease from the Browse Diseases menu or the drop-down on the top right.
Disease-associated results include:
  • Gene rankings
  • Interactive Visualisation of gene predictions on PCA/t-SNE/UMAP 2D plots
  • Exploratory & Data Analysis Plots
  • Feature importance scores by Boruta

◆ mantis-ml's predictions may also be overlapped with ranked results from independent studies (e.g. cohort-level rare-variant association studies) providing refined prioritisation of novel gene candidates based on the overlapping hypergeometric enrichment of top hits in both studies.
Validation with WES contains such results by overlapping mantis-ml's predictions against WES collapsing analysis studies in CKD, Epilepsy and ALS.
