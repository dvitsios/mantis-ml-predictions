This section contains mantis-ml's predictions for ALS, given as percentile or probability scores. The higher the percentile/probability score, the more likely a gene is to be associated with that disease).

Percentile/probability scores are given for all classifiers used with mantis-ml for stochastic semi-supervised learning.
Genes are sorted by default based on the classifier with the highest average AUC performance (ExtraTrees).
Further filtering can be applied as a follow-up, e.g. by selecting genes that are predicted as novel/positive (probability > 0.5) in at least 4 classifiers.

You can also view the distribution of AUC performance scores per classifier.
